Registration & Venue

There are 2 registration options, please choose a method that suits you from the options provided below.

1. REGISTER ONLINE

Register online securely and pay via Paypal by clicking on 'Courses'.

 

2. REGISTER IN PERSON

In order to fully register on your chosen course/s in person you must attend one of the registration dates below with your tuition fees. Registration staff will be awaiting to welcome, assess, advise and help you register.

DAY TIME
Every Mon-Fri 10am 6pm
Mon 18th - Sun 24th Dec 2017 (Male and female) 11am 7pm
Fri 22nd - Sun 24th Dec 2017 (female staff present)* 10am 7pm


* Female registration staff available between 10am-4pm
Please attempt to register on the earlier registration dates as all our courses are always on high demand

Unable to attend any of the registration dates above?

Should you genuinely be unable to register online or attend any of the registration dates above and providing that you inform us in advance, we may accept alternative methods of registration.

You may also arrange a friend or relative to register on your behalf.
