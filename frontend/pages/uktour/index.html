Welcome to the Tayyibun Institute Annual Charity
Iftar GatheringS

The Tayyibun Annual Iftar Gatherings are unmissable jam-packed fully segregated events consisting of Emaan boosting lectures, mesmerising Qur'an recitation by Quraa' from Egypt, Kuwait and Saudi Arabia, mouth-watering three course meal and charity fundraising.

Spectacular events not to be missed with world renowned scholars, Imams and teachers enlightening us with their presence and passionately moving reminders. The events will host and cater for thousands of Muslims gathered under one roof to break their fast together in the blessed month of Ramadhaan with a luxurious Iftar dinner- creating a sense of Islamic unity.

The events will be fully segregated between the genders. All in all these should be an exquisite events with the noble aim in building bonds, raising awareness and funds for our chosen charity of the year.

THIS YEARS GUEST INVITES

CONFIRMED ESTEEMED GUEST IMAMS ATTENDING IN PERSON

SHAYKH DR AL QARI ABU ABDULLAH RIYADH, SAUDI ARABIA

Shaykh Dr Al Qari Abu Abdullah graduated from Muhammad Ibn Saud University in the faculty of Usool Ud Deen and obtained a masters in Aqeedah. The Shaykh has 2 Ijazas in Qur'an. Though known for his heartfelt and emotional recitation of the Qur’an, the Shaykh is also very active in the field of Da’wah and regularly gives lectures and holds study circles in his native land while being associated with the ministry of religious affairs Riyadh Saudi Arabia.

SHAYKH DR ZUBAIR IBN MOHAMMED AYOUB MADINAH AL MUNAWARAH

Shaykh Zubair Ibn Muhammad Ayoub is an Imam from Madinah Al Munawara and the son of the world renowned Imam of the Haram Shaykh Mohammad Ayoub. ShaykhZubair is also the head of the Arabic Language faculty at the prestigious Madinah Islamic University Saudi Arabia.

SHAYKH AL QARI REDA ABDUL MUHSIN EGYPT

Shaykh Al Qari Reda Abdul Muhsin is an Imam and Khateeb in Cairo, Egypt, a Masters graduate from the most respected Islamic University in the Islamic World – Al-Azhar. A humble and yet emotive speaker he is renowned for his recitation and has Ijazat to recite in all 10 of the Qir’a’at of the Qur’an. He has lead the Tarawih and Tahajjud prayers in various mosques in Cairo for many years.

SHAYKH AL QARI ABU ABDURAHMAN RIYADH, SAUDI ARABIA

Shaykh Al Qari Abu AbdurRahman is a senior advisor to the Ministry of religious affairs of Saudi Arabia having obtained a doctorate in Shari’ah and studied under Shaykh Ibn Baz. The Shaykh is also an Imam from Riyadh as well as an active Judge for Qur'an Competitions in the blessed lands of the Haramain.

SHAYKH AL QARI ABU MOHAMMED MADINAH AL MUNAWARAH

Shaykh Al Qari Abu Mohammed is from the lands of the Haramain with a beautiful voice and superb Tajweed. The Imam is active in the field of Islamic Knowledge in the Lands of the Haramain and inspires many through motivational lectures.

THIS YEARS CHARITY

ABOUT OUR REGISTERED CHARITY PARTNER

A non-profit organisation working across the globe to strengthen humanity in the face of
poverty, social injustice and natural disaster.

Turning on our televisions, reading the newspapers or listening to the radio, it’s hard to miss the ongoing reports of famine,
poverty, war, exploitation, flooding and earthquakes that occupy the vast majority of our news.

We often get upset, feeling frustrated and helpless, wondering why little children should suffer when we live such luxurious lifestyles.
But just as often as we feel pity, we also forget.


We move on with our daily routines of work, family and leisure, pushing the images of destruction and pain to the back of our minds.
But our charity partner strives to go beyond the news reports. We strive to reach out to the mother, father, child, brother, sister,
daughter or son that doesn’t just hear the news but feels it and suffers from it everyday of their lives. Our charity partner
concentrates on the human being at the source of our daily bulletins.

As well as the distribution of emergency supplies like food, water, medical aid and shelter we also dedicate our time to long term solutions.
We want people to live healthily on their own, without the need for continuous supplies of aid. As such we create projects that will bring long term
benefits like jobs and livelihoods, ensuring that little children grow up in safe environments and with hope for the future.

Our charity partner is working to counteract the headlines rather than forget them.
We’re investing in humanity.
